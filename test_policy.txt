Security Policy Document

Access Control Policy:
- All users must authenticate with MFA
- Least privilege access enforced
- Regular access reviews conducted quarterly
- Session timeout: 30 minutes of inactivity
- Password requirements: 12+ characters, complexity rules
- Encryption at rest and in transit (AES-256)
- Zero Trust principles applied to all access decisions
- Conditional access based on device posture
- All administrative actions logged and monitored
